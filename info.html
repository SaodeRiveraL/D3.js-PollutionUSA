Datasets

The dataset is from the Kaggle Database. There are four pollutants in the dataset :NO2, CO, SO2 and O3. Each one has 5 specific columns. Observations totaled to over 1.4 million. The years included in the data are from the year 2000 to 2016. However, the year 2016 is not complete.
